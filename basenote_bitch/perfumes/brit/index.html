Burberry Brit By Burberry

This fragrance is for all the girls that plastered their dorm rooms with ‘Breakfast at Tiffany’s’ posters in college—that or some generic Eiffel Tower artwork from Ikea ahead of checkout. It’s for the girl who aspires to European travel and sophistication, but has never been. It’s Burberry Brit by Burberry baby. The scent pairs with Victoria’s Secret underwear, pearls, and fur-lined Chestnut Ugg boots. Words to describe it may be along the lines of ‘traditional, classy, and chic.’ This scent was created in 2003 during peak Chad gifting gf or mom a real cashmere Burberry scarf—and the gift that followed may have been the fashion house’s feminine fragrance offering.

Notes include lemon, frosted pear, white almond, white peony, vanilla, amber, mahogany and balsamic Tonka bean. In real life you will smell like Kate Middleton’s hat collection.
