Byredo Mojave Ghost

Are you an aspiring, minimalist Scandy betch? Have you attended any fashion week, and or, do you say the word ‘chic’ in a sartorial way? Byredo may just be your fragrance house. Kind of indie, but not really. The fragrance you want in your #topshelfie instagram that doesn’t have great longevity but looks cute AF on a mirrored tray. This is the organized desk, and marble coffee table holding a single geometric sculpture of fragrances. Also known as minimalist. Minimalism isn’t realistic and I myself like a little mess. That being said, if you want to spend $265 before taxes on a chic, minimalist scent—Byredo does have some pleasant offerings.

Pictured here, Mojave Ghost (yes these names are like album titles) has notes that include: Ambrette, Violet, Sandalwood and Chantilly Musk. In real life you will smell like a chicer version of Mark Jacobs ‘Daisy.’ Or at least a version who spent more money.
