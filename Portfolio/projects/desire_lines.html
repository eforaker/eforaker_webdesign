Desire Lines

Digital Publication, 2023

Digital booklet design of Desire Lines, an annotated essay about an alternative Metaverse proposed by German artist, Hannah Schmutterer. Typeset in both German and English. Cover designs reference cell phones, the “Metaverse glow,” Solar Punk, and Art Nouveau. Desire lines cut through both text and images of many interior spreads. The research project and digital publication was funded by Media Lab Bayern.
