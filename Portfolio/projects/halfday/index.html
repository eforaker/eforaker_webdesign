Halfday Brand Book

Branding, 2022.

Sticker illustrations, brand book design, and art direction for (sparkling probiotic iced tea beverage company) Halfday’s rebrand with Someone & Others.

Playful and summery illustrated sticker set drawn to accompany new can designs and packaging. Inspired by outdoor summer activities, 90s nostalgia, and Southern CA skate culture.

Internal brand book design to highlight the visual rebrand and clearly define Halfday’s new brand concepts. The design is collage heavy, with isolated figures, surprising scale shifts, and dynamic sticker placement. This art direction has been carried over into Halfday’s social media marketing and website design.
