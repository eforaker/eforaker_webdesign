Janky Jewels

Poster Design, 2021.

Poster design for Janky Jewels, a small New York based brand integrating sculpture, object, and costume jewelry while working towards ideas of collection and treasure.

These casino themed promotional posters use both collage and repetition within 3 columns to reference slot machines, inspired by a Janky Jewels photoshoot that took place in a casino. Bright and glittery, they are designed to be displayed in multiple and to stand out amongst the cacophony of posters in NYC.
