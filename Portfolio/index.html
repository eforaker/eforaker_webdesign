Elena Foraker

Rhode Island School of Design Graphic Design MFA, 2020. Lover of Britney Spears, basketball, reality tv, fake blood, and digital glitter. Co-founder of Studio35.

Currently—graphic designer at MARVIN magazine and freelance designer based in Providence, RI. Please reach out for commissions and collaborations via email or Instagram. Resume available upon request.

Stardust

Gif Alphabet
2020. An alphabet composed soley within Instagram stories. Each letter uses only gifs found by searching Instagram's gif library with words that begin with that letter.
