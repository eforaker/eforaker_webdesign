forbidden fruit poster

2017. A folded book comparing nutritional information and detailing cultural significance of three forbidden or taboo fruits (cherries, pomegranates, apples). Since all three fruits are particularly high in Potassium, those data points are emphasized with an apple seed, pomegranate seed, and cherry pit. When unfolded it becomes an accompanying poster featuring Rubens’ famous painting, The Fall of Man. Created for Typography 1 course at RISD.
