forbidden fruit poster

2017. A folded book comparing nutritional information and detailing cultural significance of three forbidden or taboo fruits (cherries, pomegranates, apples). All three fruits are particularly high in Potassium, so those data points are emphasized with an apple seed, pomegranate seed, and cherry pit respectively. When unfolded it becomes an accompanying poster featuring Rubens’ famous painting, "The Fall of Man."" Created for the Typography 1 course at RISD.

2017. Pages of abstracted coupons inspired by Andreas Gursky’s photograph, "99 Cent." Black and white formal studies based on the energy, rythm, and depth of his photograph are then given color and translated to the coupon format. Created for the Design Studio 1 course at RISD.

pt sans type specimen

2017. Type specimen for Paratype foundry's typeface, PT Sans. Since PT sans was specifically developed for the Russian language, this specimen features Russian proverbs, Cyrillic characters, and colors of the Russian flag. Created for the Typography 1 course at RISD.
