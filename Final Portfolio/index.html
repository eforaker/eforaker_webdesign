Home Navigation to Projects, Sketches, and About pages Main image Projects (edit, reprint) Forbidden Fruits poster (photograph) (2017) A folded book comparing nutritional information and detailing cultural significance of three forbidden or taboo fruits (cherries, pomegranates, apples). Since all three fruits are particularly high in Potassium, those data points are emphasized with an apple seed, pomegranate seed, and cherry pit. When unfolded it becomes an accompanying poster featuring Rubens’ famous painting, The Fall of Man. Created for Typography 1 course at RISD. (reprint) Steinweiss research book (photograph) (2017) A book of collected research and interviews about album cover design, Alex Steinweiss, and his influence on both the music industry and graphic design. Created for a Graduate Seminar 1 course at RISD. (edit) Studio believe posters (mockups) (2017) Series of infographic posters showing the top beliefs of Americans surveyed in 2013. Created for a Design Studio 1 course at RISD. (reprint) SPT Sans Type Specimen (photograph) (2017) XXX Jan vs Bradbury book (photograph) (2017) Typographic book comparing two quotes by famous typographers Jan Tchichold and Bradbury Thompson. The quotes were each repeatedly typeset and then interpreted both semantically and formally. Created for Typography 1 course at RISD. Petey’s Day website (animation) (2018) Interactive website depicting a day in the life of my cat, Petey Pablo. Created for Web Design course at RISD. Studio coupons (2017) Pages of abstracted coupons inspired by Andreas Gursky’s photograph, 99 Cent. Black and white formal studies based on the XXX of his photograph were then given color and translated to the coupon format. Created for a Design Studio 1 course at RISD. Fish Conspiracy (photograph) (2017) Sold at Yale’s 2017 Odds and Ends Art Book Fair.
