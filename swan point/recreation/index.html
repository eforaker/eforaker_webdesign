Established in 1846 as Swan Point Cemetary, Swan Point National Park is a meticulously maintained, 200-acre cemetery park located in Providence, Rhode Island. The beautiful grounds offer a peaceful place for visitors to camp, picnic, hike, or visit the dead. Swan Point is listed on the National Register of Historic Sites, and up until 2035 it was an actively managed, full service cemetery open to people of every faith and heritage.
