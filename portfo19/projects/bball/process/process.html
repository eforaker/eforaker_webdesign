I decided to draw this alphabet because basketball is my first love. I began by using only the lines of the court, but the result felt too anemic. I then redrew the characters using the negative shapes of the court. The chunky and diverse alphabet felt much truer to the energy and rhythm of basketball. I experimented using the characters on jerseys for my all-time favorite WNBA players (love you Sue!) and then some of my classmates. I turned the mockup jerseys into reality by silkscreening my high school basketball number on some mesh jerseys and took one out for a spin. The silkscreening was fun but couldn’t showcase the whole alphabet, so I created a final newsprint type specimen with basic play documentation.
