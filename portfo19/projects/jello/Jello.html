Inspired by Betty Friedan's revolutionary book, The Feminine Mystique, this website examines society’s impossible expectations of femininity from the 1950's until today. Elaborate jello dishes were a physical representation of the pressure to perform femininity. Today, wearing nail polish is one way to perform femininity. I reappropriated the form of mid-century recipe cards and rendered fictional jello salads the color of contemporary nail polish. The website flips to reveal a list of contemporary nail polish names.
