SVU Dead Girls is a website memorializing and archiving every woman or girl killed thus far in all twenty seasons of Law and Order: Special Victims Unit. This memorial illustrates how tired and unimaginative the Dead Girl trope is. On television, murdered women and girls are treated as props while the focus is given to the murderers or detectives. When we see girl after girl brutally killed on shows such as Law and Order, we become desensitized to violence against women.
