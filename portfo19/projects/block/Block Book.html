A 608 page hand-sewn book featuring every letter and number found on a single block in Providence. Each word or phrase is spelled out across the full page spread and is accompanied by the shape of the element it was found on (gas meter, stop sign, sticker, etc). The block was very residential and I had to peer around houses to collect the text data. The characters are skewed to demonstrate this action of viewing everything at strange angles.
