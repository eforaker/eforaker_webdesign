‡ A 608 page hand-sewn book featuring every letter and number found on a single block in Providence. Each word or phrase is spelled out across the spread and is accompanied by the shape of the material it was found on (gas meter, stop sign, sticker, etc). The block I chose is very residential and I had to peer around houses to collect the text. The letters are skewed to demonstrate this action of viewing everything at strange angles.
