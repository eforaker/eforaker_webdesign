Blimey! is a tabloid magazine for pirates. With the exception of headlines and captions, all text is sourced directly from The Buccaneers of America, a book of pirate tales originally published in 1678 by Alexandre Exquemelin. His shocking tales of pirate exploits matched the ethos of gossip magazines. Targeted advertisements are also included in the publication.
