Identity, posters, tote bags, and website designed for SMASH THE GLASS, a feminist movement I dreamt of starting after the 2016 elections. Based on the NYT opinion piece, "For Women, Glass Ceilings, and Glass Walls, Too" by president of Smith College, Kathleen McCartney, this movement highlights the way women in positions of power are held to different standards than their male counterparts. SMASH THE GLASS empowers women to forcefully break out of the boxes society puts us in.
