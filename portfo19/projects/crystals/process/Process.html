In our Graduate Form II course at RISD, we were given the assignment to explore a digital tool. I chose Illustrator’s blend tool and after experimenting I decided to create a series of forms by blending a default shape to a shape I drew. My color palette was simply instinctual, but I was reminded of gummy candy or healing crystals. I decided to create a field guide for my “crystals” so I could incorporate typography. In researching healing crystals, a clear pattern emerged of how the crystals’ powers are described and it was very fun to create phony descriptions and names for the crystals.
